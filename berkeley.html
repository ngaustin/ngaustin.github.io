Berkeley Era (2017-2022)



Berkeley Me

UC Berkeley was an illuminating experience. I initially committed as a physic major but gradually (in the most linear way possible) gravitated towards computer science. I picked up some hobbies along the way particularly during Covid, and, to be honest, I could not be happier with the decisions I made in this period of my life. Feel free to take a look at some of my milestones during this part of my journey.


Joined several urban dance teams, even leading a dance team myself.

Started dabbling in research projects. Take a look closer look over there.

Became a blogger that translated research papers to digestible, everyday reads.

Graduated in 2021 with more of my best friends.
